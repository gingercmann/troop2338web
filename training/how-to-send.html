How to Send Your Certificate

1

Log in to my.scouting.org and click the "My Training" icon.

2

On the left sidebar, select "Completed Training." Find your course and click the radio button next to it.

3

Click the "Certificate" button at the top to download your PDF.

Email your certificate to:

troop2338info@gmail.com

Pro-Tip: If the "Certificate" button doesn't open a new window, ensure your browser's pop-up blocker is disabled or try using Google Chrome/Microsoft Edge.
