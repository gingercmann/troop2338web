How to Download Your Training Certificate

1

Log in to my.scouting.org and click "My Training."

2

Select "Completed Training" and click the radio button next to your course.

3

Click "Certificate" at the top to download the PDF.

4

Ready to submit?

Now that you have your PDF saved, scroll down or click the button below to submit your documentation via our upload portal or email coordinator.

Go to Submission Section

Pro-Tip: Disable your pop-up blocker if the certificate won't download from the BSA site.
