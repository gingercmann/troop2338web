Welcome to our new website!

We're working right now to give you both a public and private portal that will serve all of our Troop needs. Thanks for your patience while we finish construction. In the meantime, all adult and youth troop leaders can get started right away.

How do I Start?

Your credentials will arrive via email. After that, the first step is to log on. You should see the "Log On" link in the upper right corner of this page. It looks like this:

When you click on that link you will be prompted to enter a User ID and password, as shown in this article of the TroopWebHost User Guide.

Can't log on? Remember we are still in transition. Everyone will onboard soon. Your Member ID will be sent to you from the webmaster when it's your turn. Please reach out to the Troop Committee through our current channels if you have any questions about credential delivery.
