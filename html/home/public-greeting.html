Welcome

If you're a Scout, Scouter, or parent in Troop 2338, you've come to the right place.

How do I Start?

Reach out to the Troop Committee to request credentials. After you receive them, the first step is to log on. You should see the "Log On" link in the upper right corner of this page. It looks like this:

When you click on that link you will be prompted to enter a User ID and password, as shown in this article of the TroopWebHost User Guide.

Can't log on? Try the "forgot username and password" link. If you're still stuck, reach out through our usual communication channels, we can help reset or restore your account.
