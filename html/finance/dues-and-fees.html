Troop 2338: Dues and Fees

Last updated: 2/14/2026

Troop 2338 offsets the cost of awards, uniforms, and activities through the following fees. These are payable for youth members only.

Fee Description Total Date(s) Due
Awards Fee $37.50 Sept 15 / Mar 15
Activity Fee $35.00 Sept 15 / Mar 15
Uniform Fee $15.00 One time (on joining)

Financial Aid: Fee assistance is available directly from Troop 2338. Please let us help if needed.

Scouting America Annual Fees

Membership fees are renewed annually by each family through my.scouting.org.

National Fee Description Cost
Youth Membership $85.00
Adult Membership $65.00
Scout Life Magazine (optional) $15.00
Merit Badge Counselor (one time) $25.00
New Member Fee (one time) $25.00

Instructions for payment come from Scouting America, not the troop.
