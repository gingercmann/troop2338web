"A Scout is Thrifty"

Fundraisers take place throughout the year, from national popcorn sales to local troop events. We track fundraising hours and use them to help offset expenses for Winter Camp and Summer Camp.

When fundraisers don't cover everything, Financial Aid is available. Every program has different guidelines; just ask an adult leader—we’re here to help you navigate the process.

  • Financial Aid from Capital Area Council
  • Scholarships from Scouting America (CAC)
  • Scholarships from Troop 2338

    If you need assistance with camp fees, uniforms, or dues, please reach out to the Scoutmaster or Committee. We will find a solution that works for your family.
