Troop 2338 Handbook & Policy

The Governing Document of our Troop

Our Troop Handbook is the primary guide for our operations, code of conduct, and local policies. To ensure it remains relevant and effective, it undergoes a rigorous annual cycle:

  • Annual Revision: Every Spring, the Committee and Scout leadership review the handbook to address new safety guidelines, troop growth, and program changes.

  • Three-Part Ratification: The final version is formally ratified by all of our Youth Leaders (PLC), Adult Leaders, and Charter Site to ensure alignment across the entire troop.

  • Member Commitment: At the start of every Scouting season, every youth member, parent, and adult leader is required to review and sign the handbook. This signature represents a binding commitment to the Scout Oath, Law, and our specific Troop policies.
