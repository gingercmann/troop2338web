Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What if I earned my merit badge at camp?

A: Your camp leader and/or Scoutmaster will receive the completed reports and enter them into the system at a later date.

Q: What if a Merit Badge counselor says they won't sign blue cards?

A: Sometimes, especially at Merit Badge Universities, a counselor will refuse to fill out a blue card. If that happens, simply ask for another record and keep it alongside any other blue card with completed sections. Send both records to Advancement for entry.

Q: Scoutbook has a blue card, and my counselor wants to use it. Will it be accepted?

A: In Troop 2338, this is a sure-fire path to success. Scoutbook generates an electronic blue card, and the counselor, Scoutmaster, and Advancement Chair can use it to follow the badge from start to finish. This is not a mandatory workflow, but it is encouraged.

Pro-Tip: Always take a photo of your completed Blue Card the moment you receive it. Physical cards can get lost, but a digital backup ensures your hard work is never forgotten!
