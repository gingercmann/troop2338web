Our Shutterfly Archives

Troop 2338 has served the youth of Georgetown, Texas since 2001.

In our early days, we used a Shutterfly Share Site to capture and preserve our history. While that platform has since closed, our photo archives live on. Today, they remain a treasured record of our past campouts, ceremonies, service projects, and more.

Contribute!

We encourage our youth historians and scribes to tell their stories as they happen — not later. Help us set that example! If you’ve taken photos at an event, find it here and upload what you’ve got. We especially appreciate captions that bring your moments to life. Video submissions are welcome too — those go in our Google Album.

Like we said, it's the Family Room. Here are three ways for you add your memories and join the conversation:

  1. Add to a current event > Click an event on our current calendar to add photos and captions. (Site members only)
  2. Click to Share on Google > Upload any photos or videos to our main Google Album. (Requires a Google Account)
  3. Click to Share on Shutterfly > Upload still photos only to share with us on Shutterfly. (Requires a Shutterfly Account)
