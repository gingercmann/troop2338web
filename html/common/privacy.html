Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2025

At Scouting America Troop 2338 Georgetown, TX (“we,” “us,” or “our”), protecting your privacy and the privacy of our youth members is a top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information through our website and related activities, consistent with Scouting America’s policies and values.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you interact with us (such as signing up for events, submitting contact forms, or joining the troop), we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and other information necessary to communicate and serve you.
  • Youth Member Information: For youth members, we collect limited information needed to support their participation and ensure their safety, always with parental or guardian consent.
  • Usage Data: We may collect non-identifying information automatically through website cookies or analytics tools to help improve our services.

How We Use Information

  • To communicate important troop news, event updates, and safety information.
  • To administer troop activities, registrations, and volunteer coordination.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements related to youth programs.
  • To improve our website and services based on user feedback and analytics.

Sharing Your Information

  • We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.
  • We may share information with Scouting America or other affiliated organizations as necessary to support program delivery and compliance.
  • We may share limited information with trusted service providers (such as web hosting or email services) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Photos or videos featuring individuals are shared with media, including websites, newsletters, and social media, only after obtaining a signed talent release form from the individuals or their legal guardians.
  • In emergencies or legal situations, we may disclose information as required by law.

Your Choices and Rights

  • You can opt out of receiving promotional communications at any time by using the unsubscribe link or contacting us directly.
  • Parents or guardians may review, correct, or request deletion of their youth member’s personal information by contacting us.
  • We take reasonable steps to secure your personal data but cannot guarantee complete security.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies only to enhance user experience and gather anonymous usage statistics. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the site may not work properly.
